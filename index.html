Writings

Once there was a beautiful garden that was located near a very spooky house, where a few children used to play.One evening the children decided to explore the spooky house,so they cautiously opened the creepy squeaky door. Once they were inside, the door shut behind them! They started to wander around, seeing that they had nothing else to do.They saw amazing artifacts and wondrous treasures. They were dazzled by what they saw. The spooky house wasn't as spooky on the inside. A little while later, the children started to hear clunky noises and creepy sounds.They carefully approached the noise. They saw a clunky old functioning robot setting a wonderful large meal in the most extraordinary room. After the robot finished it asked”Are you hungry? If you are then please eat. I made this meal especially for you.” The children had so many questions. How did the robot get there? How did the robot know they were there? Was the robot kind? For one moment they had to forget their questions because they were hungry. And when you are hungry you have to eat. After they ate the delicious meal they said thank you to the robot and left.But they promised the robot they would visit again.
